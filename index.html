Mobile puzzle game

In this game the goal is to get the biljard ball to the pocket at the end of the level. Along the way certain obstacles can be found such as bumpers, moving blocks and speed boosts. Various sound effects along with 3 uplifing songs can be heared when playing the game. All the coding and artwork for this game were created by me except for the sound effects and music.

Cartoony water shader

A GLSL shader

This project showcases a watershader in GLSL for Unity. This water shader has multiple features. The foam strength can be adjusted at runtime along with the foam generated when the water collides with a surface. Also the wave frequency and length can be modified to mimic calm waters or even stormy weather. I learned shading in HLSL and wanted to know how to create custom shaders in Unity (meaning GLSL). This project is my first ever shader in Unity.

Created during my final year at DAE
